About Me

I'm a software developer in-training based out of Minneapolis Minnesota.

Since 1992, I've been interested in how and why things work. Throughout my life, I've held different jobs to acquire knowledge in every area I possibly could. I've worked in numerous areas including: hardscaping, AVL integration, restaurants, cabinetry, delivery, shipping and distribution, and several others. What persisted throughout was my curiosity about computers and the mechanics underlying them. I am currently enrolled in a software development bootcamp, and I'm loving every minute of it! I plan to use everything I've accumulated over the years to better myself and my family.
