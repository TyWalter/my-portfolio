About Me

I'm a software developer in-training based out of Minneapolis Minnesota.

Since 1992, I've been interested in how things work and why things work. For most of my life, I've held different jobs to gain knowledge in every area I possibly could. I've worked in a lot of areas including: hardscaping, AVL integration, restaurants, cabinetry, delivery positions, shipping and distribution, and others. Every single job I've had has given me the working knowledge on how to do that job properly and with it, I plan to use everything I've accumulated to better my family and myself. Throughout everything I've learned along the way, the one thing that kept coming back was my interest in computers and how they work.
